# Hansa Alignment Plan

## Current Verdict

The codebase has moved onto the Hansa track: authored map, Hansa routes, Kontore,
league actions, rulers, commanders and chronicle flavour. The remaining alignment
work is about making trade control feel like the main contest.

## Keep Tightening

- **Buildings:** favour Rathaus, Speicher, Salzspeicher, Brick-Gothic Church,
  Guildhall, Counting House, Shipyard, City Walls, Roland Statue and Kontor
  language over broad fantasy city upgrades.
- **Resources:** add salt, amber, herring/stockfish, cloth, beer and wax where
  they create contested routes.
- **Armies:** keep numbers small and expensive. Use "town levies", "hired men" and
  commanders to fit the merchant-league frame.
- **Sea power:** make the Sound, Gotland waters, Gulf of Riga and Gulf of Finland
  visible strategic spaces.
- **Victory:** make League/trade control the long-term direction, with domination
  and prestige as secondary endings.

## Highest-Value Next Slice

Build route conflict before adding more content rows. The game needs a player to
feel: "that toll, blockade or embargo changed my plan." Once that works, extra
goods and buildings have a strong reason to exist.
