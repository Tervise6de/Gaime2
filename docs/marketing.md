# Sea of Coin Marketing Notes

## Positioning

Sea of Coin is a compact browser strategy game about medieval trade power
around the Baltic and North Sea. Build routes, control Kontore, steer the
League, tax the Sound, embargo rivals and use war as an economic weapon.

## Core Promise

Win the merchant sea. Every route is a political commitment, every toll creates
enemies, and every city you control changes the balance of the League.

## Differentiation

- More specific than a broad 4X.
- Faster and lighter than a grand-strategy campaign.
- More strategic than a simple idle/economy web game.
- Strong visual hook: an authored Hansa map with ports, cities, sea lanes and
  trade goods.

## Copy Direction

Avoid "rule a kingdom" language. The fantasy is merchant power, league politics
and Baltic pressure, not a standard crown-and-castle conquest game.
